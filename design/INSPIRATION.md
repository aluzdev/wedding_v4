# Inspiration — perplexity.ai/personal-computer

Captured 2026-06-12 → `design/inspiration/` (13 desktop section shots, mobile full, tokens.json).

## Register
Cinematic, hushed, premium. Lush dark botanical imagery (mossy hills, hydrangeas, glass orbs) alternating with warm paper-cream sections. Quiet confidence; almost no UI chrome.

## Palette (from tokens.json + shots)
- Cream surfaces: `#FCFCF9` / `#FAF8F5` / `#F3F0EC` (warm paper, not pure white)
- Dark surfaces: `#121211` / `#1E1D1C` / `#242322` (warm near-black, never #000 flat)
- Ink on cream: `#27251E`
- Accents: muted teal (eyebrow labels), warm amber/gold (voice UI dots)
- Frosted-glass pills/cards: white at low alpha + heavy backdrop blur over imagery

## Typography
- Display: transitional serif (PPLX Serif ≈ Georgia family), weight 400, large (52px h1 / 30px h2), centered
- Body: compact grotesque sans, small (12–14px), generous leading
- Eyebrows: tiny uppercase, letterspaced, accent-colored (e.g. "MODEL ORCHESTRATION")

## Layout patterns per section
1. Hero: full-bleed dark botanical image, centered serif headline + one-line sub, frosted-glass pill (input/CTA), two pill buttons
2. Feature scenes: dark, sticky/centered glass card demos, small left-aligned title + 2-line sub in corner
3. Cream interlude: centered eyebrow + serif headline + radial diagram, lots of air
4. Dark capability grid: eyebrow + serif h2, 3×2 icon cards, tiny sans descriptions
5. Big centered statement: "One shortcut. Total context." serif over dark
6. Closing: full-bleed flowers, serif "Available now.", glass pill CTA, fine-print line
7. Footer: single thin line, tiny links

## Motion
Soft fades and scroll-settled scenes; glass blur transitions; nothing bouncy. Content readable without animation.

## What makes it distinctive
Serif elegance floating over dark living botanicals with frosted-glass UI — translates 1:1 to a garden-venue wedding: same registry of calm luxury, swap product copy for names/date/RSVP.

## Translation guardrails
No Perplexity assets, copy, logo, or asterisk mark. Adapt: dark-garden hero + cream info sections + glass RSVP pill + serif couple names.
